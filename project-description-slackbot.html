SLACK ROBOT

Project description SLACK Robot for ATRIUM CMDB

Background

In our work environment we utilize a CMDB for obvious purposes. Years ago the choice was made to use BMC Atrium CMDB and it works very well for its purpose.

However when it comes to extracting the data for data quality purposes or supplying our stakeholders with data, the solution was limited.

Therefore I've build this bot to overcome these two pain points.

This bot is build to automate a manual health-check procedure that was used by one of our vendors.
The vendor used to work with a check list in the morning to verify if data quality and data integrity was up to par.

Second benefit is that the introduction of this bot made the information in the CMDB available on-the-fly and whenever the stakeholder needs it.

We removed 30 minutes of paid services time on a daily basis and introduced a lot of flexibility for our stakeholders.
